#What is BudgetBuddy?
BudgetBuddy is a friendly finance tracking app that helps you take control of your money without the stress. Whether you want to save for something special, pay off debt, or just understand where your money goes each month, BudgetBuddy makes budgeting simple, visual, and even enjoyable.

#Screens and Features
#Welcome Screen
This is where your journey begins. You can either create a new account or sign in if you're already a member. It's a warm, simple entry point to the app.

#Registration Screen
Creating an account takes less than two minutes. Just enter your name, email, and password, and you're ready to start tracking your finances.

Login Screen
Access your account securely with your email and password. Your financial data stays private and protected.

Forgot Password Screen
Can't remember your password? No problem. Enter your email, and we'll send you a reset link to get back into your account quickly.

Dashboard Screen
This is your financial home base. You'll see your total balance, total income, and total expenses at a glance. Quick action buttons let you add expenses or set budgets. Your five most recent transactions are also displayed right here.

Expense Category Screen
Choose from categories like Food, Transportation, Shopping, Entertainment, Bills, Healthcare, and Education. This helps you organize your spending and see where your money really goes.

Add Expenses Screen
Log a purchase in seconds. Enter the amount, write a short description, pick a category, and save. That's it - your expense is recorded.

View All Expenses Screen
See every single transaction you've ever made. You can search through past expenses, filter by category, or edit and delete entries if you make a mistake.

Monthly Budget Screen
Set spending limits for each category that matters to you. See how much you have left throughout the month and stay on track with your financial goals.

Log Monthly Budget Screen
Record your monthly budget limits for different categories. This is where you tell the app how much you plan to spend on food, transport, shopping, and more each month.

Savings Goals Screen
Dreaming of a vacation, new laptop, or emergency fund? Create savings goals here. Name your goal, set the target amount, and choose a deadline. Watch your progress grow as you save.

Log Savings Goals Screen
Add money toward your savings goals over time. Update your progress and see how close you are to reaching each goal. Every small contribution adds up.

Statistics Screen
Beautiful charts show you exactly where your money is going. A bar chart breaks down spending by category. A bar chart shows your daily or monthly patterns. Filter by different time periods to spot trends.

Budget Health Screen
Get a health score from 0 to 100 based on your spending and saving habits. Scores above 80 mean you're doing great. Scores below 40 mean it's time to review. You'll also get personalized tips to improve your financial wellness.

Profile Screen
Manage your account details, view your information, and log out when you're done. This is your personal command center.

Notification Screen
See all your alerts in one place. BudgetBuddy notifies you when you're reaching your spending limits, when you hit savings milestones, and sends weekly summaries to keep you informed.
